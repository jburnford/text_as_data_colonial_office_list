UGANDA

The provisional agreement reached at the Uganda Constitutional Conference, 1961, that Uganda should attain independence in 1962 was confirmed at the Uganda Independence Conference held in London in June 1962, under the chairmanship of the Secretary of State for the Colonies.

Uganda became independent and a member of the Commonwealth on 9th October 1962.

The Secretary of State for Commonwealth Relations is now the channel of communication between Her Majesty's Government in the United Kingdom and Her Majesty's Government in Uganda.

Basic information about Uganda can be found in the 1962 edition of the Colonial Office List.
