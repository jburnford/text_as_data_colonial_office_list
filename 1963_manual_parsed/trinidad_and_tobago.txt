TRINIDAD AND TOBAGO

Following a general election in Trinidad and Tobago full internal self-government was attained in December 1961.

Agreement was reached at a Conference held in London May-June 1962, under the chairmanship of the Secretary of State and the Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for the Colonies, and Trinidad and Tobago attained independence and became a member of the Commonwealth on 31st August 1962.

The Secretary of State for Commonwealth Relations is now the channel of communication between Her Majesty's Government in the United Kingdom and Her Majesty's Government in Trinidad and Tobago.

Basic information about Trinidad and Tobago can be found in the 1962 edition of the Colonial Office List.
