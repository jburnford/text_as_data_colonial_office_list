SOMALILAND PROTECTORATE

The Somaliland Protectorate became independent on 26th June 1960 and on 1st July 1960 united with the former Trust Territory of Somalia to form the Somali Republic. Responsibility for Her Majesty’s Government’s relations with the Somali Republic rests with the Foreign Office.

Basic information about the Somaliland Protectorate will be found in the 1960 edition of the Colonial Office List.
