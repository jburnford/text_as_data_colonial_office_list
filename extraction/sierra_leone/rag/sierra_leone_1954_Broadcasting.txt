Broadcasting

There is no direct broadcasting, but Government operates a wired broadcasting system in Freetown to which there were 3,595 subscribers in August 1953. In the same month the number of licences issued to owners of private sets was 818. The operation and maintenance of the station is in charge of the Posts and Telegraphs Department while the rediffusion programmes are arranged by the Public Relations Department.
