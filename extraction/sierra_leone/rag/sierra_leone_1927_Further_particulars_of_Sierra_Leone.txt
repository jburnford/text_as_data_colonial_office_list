#### Further particulars of Sierra Leone

For further particulars of Sierra Leone, see "The Handbook of Sierra Leone," by T. N. Goddard, published for the Sierra Leone Government by Messrs. Grant Richards Ltd., London, W. C. 2. See also H. C. Luke's "Bibliography of Sierra Leone," 2nd edition, Oxford University Press, 1925.
