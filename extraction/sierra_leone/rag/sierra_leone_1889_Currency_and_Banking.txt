**Currency and Banking.**

Besides British currency, gold doubloons, eagles, and the coins of the Latin union, are current and legal tender. There is no Colonial coinage, and no note circulation. No private banks exist; but a Government Savings Bank was established in 1882, and had in 1887 £9,331.1., deposited, belonging to 501 depositors.
