**Labour**

The Labour Department is responsible for the supervision of all labour matters, including the promotion of good relations between employers and workers, the setting up of wage negotiating machinery both voluntary and statutory, conciliation in trade disputes, the placing of workers in employment through the medium of employment exchanges, the registration (by photographs and finger printing) of workers in certain areas, the encouragement and development of trade unions, arrangements for trade testing, the collation of statistics, and various forms of labour inspection.

The Department also includes a Technical Training Centre which provides training for semi-skilled workers.
