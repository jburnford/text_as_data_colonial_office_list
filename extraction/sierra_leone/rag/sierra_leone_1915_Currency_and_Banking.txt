Currency and Banking.

Besides British currency, the 5 franc pieces of the Latin union are legal tender, and the new W.A. currency was put into circulation in 1913. The Bank of British West Africa has two branches in the Colony and some agencies in the Protectorate. A Government Savings Bank was established in 1882, and had invested by the end of 1913, the sum of 113,359£.
