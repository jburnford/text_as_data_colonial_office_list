Defence.

The head-quarters of Her Majesty's Forces on the West Coast of Africa, consisting of four companies of a West India Regiment (400 men), are stationed at Sierra Leone. There is also an establishment for victualling and coaling ships of the Royal Navy. Batteries armed with heavy guns are in course of erection for the defence of Freetown harbour.
