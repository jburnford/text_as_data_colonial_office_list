Posts and Telegraphs

There are 80 post offices and postal agencies in the Colony and Protectorate but money order and savings bank business is conducted at the larger offices only.

There are about 2,000 miles of telephone and telegraph lines in operation, the former operated by the Post Office and the latter by the Sierra Leone Railway. A trunk telephone line to link Bo with Freetown is now being constructed.

Wireless stations have been installed at several stations in the Protectorate and at Freetown a radio beacon for shipping is in operation.
