Colony: Sierra Leone
Year: 1954
Section: Health
Type: narrative
---

In 1952 beds available in hospitals (including two Infiraries and a maternity hospital) totalled 1,140 (Government 849, Missions 193, Mining 98). In-patients at Government Hospitals numbered 10,119 and out-patients 124,800. There were 28 Government dispensaries (excluding 20 treatment centres attached to the Endemic Diseases Control Unit) at which 126,601 new cases were handled. Special measures to control malaria and endemic diseases are undertaken by Government.

Actual expenditure on medical and allied services in 1952 was £335,954 from Colony resources and £39,562 from Colonial Development and Welfare Funds. By comparison actual expenditure in 1935 was £63,923.
