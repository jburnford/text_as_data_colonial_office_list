In 1956, £5,356,772 of total revenue was derived from Customs and £2,441,285 from taxes. In the same year, the main heads of expenditure were:

- Development Schemes (excluding transfers of £812,000 to the Development and Education Funds from revenue) £1,191,274,
- Medical and Education Services £1,569,928,
- Public Works and Electricity recurrent £982,630,
- non-recurrent Public Works £460,156,
- Law and Order £623,139,
- Provincial Administration and the Secretariat £183,986.
