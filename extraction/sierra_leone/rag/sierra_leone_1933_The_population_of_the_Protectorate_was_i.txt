The population of the Protectorate was in 1931 estimated at 1,672,057 and is composed of the following tribes:

Northern Province { Tonnes, Limbas, Susus,
Lokkos, Fulahs, Mandingoes, Korankos, Yalunkas.
Mendes, Sherbos, Konnos,
Southern Province { Krima, Gallinas, Golas,
Vais, a few Kissis.
