PUBLIC FINANCE

(See Note on page 27.)

In 1950 Revenue amounted to £3,268,530 and Expenditure to £2,978,819. The public debt stood at £3,448,841. The main head of expenditure was £695,616 on Development Schemes. Provincial Administration cost £124,734, £233,959 was spent on Medical Services, £62,539 on Agriculture and £185,559 on Education.
