The rates of postage are as follows:

| United Kingdom and d. | Postal Union and Non-Union Countries |
|-----------------------|-------------------------------------|
| Letters per 4 oz.     | Newspapers and other articles per 2 ozs. |

The Parcel Post system has been introduced, and parcels up to 11 lbs. in weight can be posted. Rate 9d. per lb.
