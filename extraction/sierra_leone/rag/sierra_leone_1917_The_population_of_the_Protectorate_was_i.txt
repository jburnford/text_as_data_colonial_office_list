The population of the Protectorate was in 1911 estimated at 1,327,560 and is composed of the following tribes:

- **Karene District**
  - Temnes, Limbas, Susua, Lokkos, Foulahs, Mandingoes.
- **Ronietta** (including Yonnis), Sherbro.
- **Railway** (Mendes, Konnohs, a few Kissis).
- **Koinadugu**
  - Korankas, Yalunkas, Limbas, Foulahs, Mandingoes.
- **Northern Sherbro District**
  - Sherbro, Mendes, Krimis, Gallinas and Veis.
