The population of the Protectorate was in 1911 estimated at 1,327,560 and is composed of the following tribes:

- **Karene District**
  - Temnes, Limbas, Susus, Lokkos, Foulahs, Mandingooses.
- **Ronietta**
  - Mendes, Temnes (including Yonnies), Sherbros.
- **Railway**
  - Mendes, Konnohas, a few Kissis.
- **Koinadugu**
  - Korankos, Yalunkas, Limbas, Foulahs, Mandingooses.
- **Northern Sherbro**
  - Sherbros, Mendes, Krimas, Gallinas and Veis.
