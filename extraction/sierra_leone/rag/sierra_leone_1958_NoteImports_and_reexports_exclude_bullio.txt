Note.—Imports and re-exports exclude bullion and specie. Domestic exports include locally produced gold.

In 1938, 68·8% of total imports came from the United Kingdom, 17·1% from other Commonwealth countries and 14·1% from foreign countries. The corresponding figures in 1956 were: 54·2%, 17·2% and 28·6%.

In 1938 61·5% of total exports went to the United Kingdom, 1·3% to other Commonwealth countries, and 37·2% to foreign countries. The corresponding figures in 1956 were: 64·1%, 1·7% and 34·2%.
