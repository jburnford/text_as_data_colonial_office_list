Colony: Sierra Leone
Year: 1950
Section: Roads
Type: narrative
---

Up to 31st December, 1948, there were 1,184 miles of first-class roads in the Protectorate maintained by the Public Works Department, and 430 miles of lower standard maintained by local authorities. In addition approximately 90 miles of roads were maintained by private companies.

In the Colony Peninsula there are 161 miles of roads maintained by the Public Works Department of which 82 miles are bitumen surfaced.
