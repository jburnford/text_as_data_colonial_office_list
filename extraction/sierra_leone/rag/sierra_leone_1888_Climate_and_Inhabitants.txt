Climate and Inhabitants.

The climate is fairly healthy during the dry season. The mean temperature is 75°, the annual range being from 63° to 94°. The rainfall is about 44 inches, the rainy season being from June to October, and the cool Harmattan from November to February.

The inhabitants are most of the negro race, the chief tribes being the Jollofs, Mandingoes, and Jollohs. There are about 25 Europeans in the colony, out of a population, in 1881, of 14,150. The town of Bathurst contains about 6,000 inhabitants.
