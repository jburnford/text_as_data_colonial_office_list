Currency and Banking.

Besides British currency, gold doubloons, eagles, and the coins of the Latin union, are current and legal tender. There is no Colonial coinage, and no note circulation. There is no bank in the Colony.
A Government Savings Bank was established in 1882, and had in 1892 £6,817 deposited, belonging to 1,729 depositors.
