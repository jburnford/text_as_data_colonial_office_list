Customs.

The import duties, which make provision for preferential treatment to British goods, are mainly ad valorem (20 per cent. from the British Empire and 30 per cent. from Foreign Countries); but many important articles such as Tobacco, Spirits, Wines, Beer, Petroleum products, Salt, &c., are liable to a specific duty. There are also export duties on Palm Kernels (£1 10s. 0d. a ton), Kola nuts (½d. a lb.), and Gum Copal (3d. a lb.).
