**Currency and Banking.**

Besides British currency, gold doubloons, eagles, and the coins of the Latin union are legal tender. There is no Colonial coinage, and no note circulation. The Bank of British West Africa has a branch in the Colony. A Government Savings Bank was established in 1882, and had invested by the end of 1910, the sum of 90,162L.
