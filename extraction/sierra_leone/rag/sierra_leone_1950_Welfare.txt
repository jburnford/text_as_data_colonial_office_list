**Welfare**

The Social Welfare Department deals with individual cases of matrimonial difficulty, illegitimacy, maintenance, guardianship and difficult children including juvenile delinquents. An Approved School was opened in 1947.

The Department runs the Freetown Community Centre which now serves 1,119 group activities embracing some 28,760 persons. During 1948, 40 Youth Organisations were registered with the Department with a total membership of 3,093 and an age range of 10 to 40 years. The groups' interests are mainly religious, social, literary and handicrafts. The Boy Scouts, Girl Guides and Boys' Brigade Movements are active in the Colony and the Protectorate.

Work with Women and Youth Clubs was started in the Colony Rural Areas. A Welfare Centre was opened at Lunsar and a similar one for women and children started at Marampa at the request of the management of the Mines.
