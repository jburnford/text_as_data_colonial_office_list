Colony: Gold Coast
Year: 1923
Section: Harbours.
Type: narrative
---

The Colonial Bank began business at Accra in 1917, and branches have now been opened at other centres. Both Banks have a savings department. In 1888, a Government Savings Bank was established which is now under Post Office management. On 31st March, 1922, the amount to the credit of depositors was 52,086L.

Accounts are kept in sterling.
