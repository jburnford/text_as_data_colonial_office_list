Principal Occupations

Most of the population is engaged in agriculture. The estimated numbers in other occupations are:

| Occupation          | Number |
|---------------------|--------|
| Government Service  | 5,640   |
| Building            | 90      |
| Distributive Trades | 5,982   |
| Shipping            | 77      |
| Others              | 404     |

There are three Trade Unions—the Gambia Labour Union, the Gambia Workers Union and the River Trade and Dealers Union (formerly the River Trade and Commercial Workers Union).
