Currency and Banking.

The legal tender and usual currency is that of the Latin Union; there is no Colonial coinage and no note issue. A Government savings bank was established in 1886, and had on 31st Dec., 1897, 2,562l. deposited by 169 depositors, in 1898, 3,822l.
