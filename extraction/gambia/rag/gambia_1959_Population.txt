Population

The latest estimate of the total population is 275,100, composed as follows:

- Colony—Bathurst—21,000; Kombo St. Mary—7,800; Protectorate—246,300. There are also about 10,000 seasonal immigrants. The population of Bathurst is African except for a few hundred Europeans, Syrians and Lebanese. The main tribal groups in the Protectorate are:

| Approximate Nos. |
|------------------|
| Mandingo         | 103,000 |
| Fula (all tribal subdivisions) | 57,000 |
| Wolof            | 36,000 |
| Serahuli         | 20,000 |
| Jola             | 21,000 |
| Others (of less than 5,000 each) | 10,000 |

The Principal Towns are—

Colony

Bathurst—capital; population 21,000.

Protectorate

Western Division—Brikama.
Central Division—Mansa Konko.
MacCarthy Island Division—Georgetown.
Upper River Division—Basse.

Population in the Protectorate is evenly distributed and there are no towns of over 3,000 inhabitants.
