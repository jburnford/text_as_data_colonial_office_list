Population

The total population is estimated at 273,261, composed as follows:

- Colony—Bathurst (1951 Census) 19,602; Kombo St. Mary (1951 Census) 7,695; Protectorate (1955 estimate) 245,964. The population of Bathurst is African except for a few hundred Europeans, Syrians and Lebanese. The main tribal groups in the Protectorate are:
  - Mandingo: 106,589
  - Fula (all tribal subdivisions): 56,426
  - Wolof: 31,183
  - Serahuli: 20,844
  - Jola: 19,322
  - Others (of less than 5,000 each): 11,600

The Principal Towns are—

Colony
Bathurst—capital.

Protectorate
Western Division—Brikama.
Central Division—Mansa Konko.
MacCarthy Island Division—Georgetown.
Upper River Division—Basse.

Population in the Protectorate is evenly distributed and there are no towns of over 3,000 inhabitants.
