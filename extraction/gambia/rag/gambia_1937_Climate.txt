**Climate.**
The climate of the Gambia during the dry season from the end of November to the middle of May is pleasant and generally healthy. During the wet season—June to October—the conditions are much the same as elsewhere on the West Coast of Africa: the change from excessive dryness to a damp atmosphere is found to be trying. In the interior the temperature is high from March to June. The lowest reading of the thermometer in the shade during 1934 in Bathurst was 55° F. in June, the highest recorded temperature being 103° F. in May.

The rainfall during the year 1934 in Bathurst was 44.01 inches. The rainfall varies considerably; the average is 47 inches.
