Foreign Consuls.

United States.

Norway, P. Bourquin.

All European officers are provided either with furnished quarters, on which they pay 2½ per cent. on the value of the furniture, or with lodging allowance, calculated at the rate of 10 per cent. of their salary, but not exceeding 80l. per annum.
