**Principal Occupations**

Most of the population is engaged in agriculture. The estimated numbers in other occupations in 1953 were:

| Occupation       | Number |
|------------------|--------|
| Government Service | 2,318  |
| Building          | 103    |
| Distributive Trades | 1,449  |
| Transport         | 186    |
| Shipping          | 361    |
| Others            | 340    |

There are three registered Trade Unions—Bathurst Trade Union, Gambia Labour Union and Gambia River Trade and Commercial Workers' Union.
