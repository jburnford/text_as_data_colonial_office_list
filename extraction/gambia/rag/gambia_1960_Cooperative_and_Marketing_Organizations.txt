Co-operative and Marketing Organizations

The Gambia Oilseeds Marketing Board handles the export of all groundnuts and other oilseeds. Its aims are to ensure orderly marketing, to improve the quality of exports, and from its profits to finance schemes of research and development. A Cattle Marketing Board was set up in 1955. A Registrar of Co-operative Societies, an Assistant Registrar, a Trainee Assistant Registrar and six Inspectors have been appointed. There are at present 48 societies including 9 unregistered primaries and 3 unregistered secondaries with a total membership of 3,188.
