The principal towns are—

**Colony**

Bathurst—capital—population 19,602.

**Protectorate**

Western Division—Brikama.
Central Division—Mansa Konko.
MacCarthy Island Division—Georgetown.
Upper River Division—Basse.

Population in the Protectorate is evenly distributed and there are no towns of over 3,000 inhabitants.
