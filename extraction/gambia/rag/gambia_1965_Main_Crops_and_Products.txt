Colony: Gambia
Year: 1965
Section: Main Crops and Products
Type: narrative
---



The main crops are groundnuts for export (75,000 tons of undecorticated nuts were purchased in 1963-64) rice, sorghum and millet; some maize and a little cotton for village weaving are also grown.



The livestock census results (1962-63) were:—cattle 182,006; sheep 53,991; goats 93,955; pigs 1,999; horses 51; donkeys 2,000 approximately.



A fair amount of fish is caught in coastal waters and some in the lower river; very little however is done farther up-river, though fish are fairly plentiful. The value of smoked fish exported to Sierra Leone in 1963 was £34,000.
