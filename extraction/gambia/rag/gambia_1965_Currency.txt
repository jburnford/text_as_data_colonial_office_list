Colony: Gambia
Year: 1965
Section: Currency
Type: narrative
---



The currency in circulation is issued by the West African Currency Board in London. It consists of notes of £5, £1 and 10s.; yellow metal coins of 2s., 1s., and 6d.; cupro-nickel coins of 3d.; bronze coins of 1d. and ½d. The currency is interchangeable with sterling at par. Gambian Currency notes replaced West African notes late in 1964.
