Currency and Banking.

The legal tender and usual currency is that of the Latin Union; there is no Colonial coinage and no note issue. A Government savings bank was established in 1886, and had on 31st Dec., 1903, 5,382£, deposited by 349 persons. A Branch of the Bank of British West Africa was opened in January, 1902.
