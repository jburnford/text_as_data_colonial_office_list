Principal Occupations

Most of the population is engaged in agriculture. The estimated numbers in other occupations in 1952 were:

| Occupation          | Number |
|---------------------|--------|
| Government Service  | 3,050  |
| Building            | 105    |
| Distributive Trades | 1,360  |
| Transport           | 200    |
| Shipping            | 350    |
| Others              | 380    |

There are three registered Trade Unions—Bathurst Trade Union, Gambia Labour Union and Gambia River Trade and Commercial Workers’ Union.
