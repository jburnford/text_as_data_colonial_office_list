Co-operative and Marketing Organizations

The Gambia Oilseeds Marketing Board handles the export of all groundnuts and other oilseeds. Its aims are to ensure orderly marketing, to improve the quality of exports, and from its profits to finance schemes of research and development. A Cattle Marketing Board was set up in 1955. There has been a steady increase in the development of Farmers' Marketing Co-operatives since the registration of the first societies in 1956 and there are now 47 registered societies and 8 under supervision.
