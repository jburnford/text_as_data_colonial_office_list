Population
The latest estimate of the total population is 283,761 composed as follows:

- Colony—Bathurst—23,655; Kombo St. Mary—9,286; Protectorate—250,820. In 1960 there were 4,941 seasonal immigrants. The population of Bathurst is African except for a few hundred Europeans, Syrians and Lebanese. The main tribal groups in the Protectorate are:

| Approximate Nos. |
|------------------|
| Mandingo         | 102,000 |
| Fula (all tribal subdivisions) | 47,000 |
| Wolof            | 28,000 |
| Serahuli         | 23,000 |
| Jola             | 20,000 |
| Jomboonko        | 6,000 |
| Lorobo           | 6,000 |
| Others (of less than 5,000) | 19,000 |

The Principal Towns are—

**Colony**
- Bathurst—capital; population 23,655.

**Protectorate**
- Western Division—Brikama.
- Lower River Division—Mansa Konko.
- MacCarthy Island Division—Georgetown.
- Upper River Division—Basse.

Population in the Protectorate is evenly distributed and there are no towns of over 3,000 inhabitants.
