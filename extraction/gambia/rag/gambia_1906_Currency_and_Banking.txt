Currency and Banking.

The legal tender and usual currency is that of the Latin Union; there is no Colonial coinage and no note issue. A Government savings bank was established in 1896, and had on 31st Dec., 1904, 3,060£., deposited by 376 persons. A Branch of the Bank of British West Africa was opened in January, 1902.
