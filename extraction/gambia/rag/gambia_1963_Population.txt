Population

The estimated population at mid-1961 was 287,300 composed as follows:—Bathurst—24,500; Kombo St. Mary—9,600; Protectorate—253,200. In 1961 there were 4,330 seasonal immigrants. The population of Bathurst is African except for a few hundred Europeans, Syrians and Lebanese. The main tribal groups in the Protectorate are:

| Approximate Nos. |
|------------------|
| Mandingo         | 102,000 |
| Fula (all tribal sub-divisions) | 60,200 |
| Wolof            | 28,000 |
| Serahuli         | 24,000 |
| Jola             | 20,000 |
| Others (of less than 5,000) | 19,000 |

The Principal Towns are—

Colony

Bathurst—capital; population 24,500.

Protectorate

Western Division—Brikama.
Lower River Division—Mansa Konko.
MacCarthy Island Division—Georgetown.
Upper River Division—Basse.

Population in the Protectorate is evenly distributed and there are no towns of over 3,000 inhabitants.
