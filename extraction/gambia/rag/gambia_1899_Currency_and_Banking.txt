Currency and Banking.

The legal tender and usual currency is that of the Latin Union; there is no Colonial coinage and no note issue. A Government savings bank was established in 1886, and had on 31st Dec., 1895, £2,563.1. deposited by 138 depositors, in 1896, £1,988.1. deposited by 154 depositors, but there are no private banks.
