### Currency.

The currency in circulation is issued by the West African Currency Board in London on behalf of the British West African territories. It consists of notes of 100s., 20s. and 10s.; yellow metal alloy coins of 2s., 1s. and 6d.; bronze coins of 1d., and ½d. and nickel coins of one-tenth penny. The currency is interchangeable with sterling at par.
