Colony: Gambia
Year: 1965
Section: Co-operative and Marketing Organizations
Type: narrative
---



The Gambia Oilseeds Marketing Board handles the export of all groundnuts and other oilseeds. Its aims are to ensure orderly marketing and to improve the quality of exports. The progressive development of Farmers' Marketing Co-operatives continues. The first society was registered in 1956; there are now 83 registered societies which handled 15·2% of the 1963/64 groundnut crop.
