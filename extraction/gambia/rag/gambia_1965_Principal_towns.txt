Principal towns



The only town is Bathurst (27,809) which is the seat of Government, the commercial centre and the chief port. Other centres, none of which exceeds 5,000 inhabitants, are the four divisional headquarters shown below and the river ports of Kaur and Kuntaur.



| Division       | Population | Headquarters |

|----------------|------------|--------------|

| Western        | 55,393     | Brikama      |

| Lower River    | 97,272     | Mansakonko   |

| MacCarthy Island | 64,755   | Georgetown   |

| Upper River    | 58,049     | Basse        |
