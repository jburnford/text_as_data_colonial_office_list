Population

The total population is estimated at 280,531, composed as follows:

- Colony—Bathurst (1951 Census) 19,602; Kombo St. Mary (1951 Census) 7,695; Protectorate (1954 estimate) 253,234. The population of Bathurst is African except for a few hundred Europeans, Syrians and Lebanese. The main tribal groups in the Protectorate are:
  - Mandingo 106,606
  - Fula (all tribal subdivisions) 57,452
  - Wolof 35,670
  - Serahuli 21,153
  - Jola 19,750
  - Others (of less than 5,000 each) 12,603

The Principal Towns are—

Colony
- Bathurst—capital—population 19,602.

Protectorate
- Western Division—Brikama.
- Central Division—Mansa Konko.
- MacCarthy Island Division—Georgetown.
- Upper River Division—Basse.

Population in the Protectorate is evenly distributed and there are no towns of over 3,000 inhabitants.
