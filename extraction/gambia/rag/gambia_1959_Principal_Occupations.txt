Principal Occupations

Most of the population is engaged in agriculture. The estimated numbers in other occupations are:

- Government Service ... 2,700
- Building ... 280
- Distributive Trades ... 2,450
- Shipping ... 290
- Others ... 310

There are three registered Trade Unions—Bathurst Trade Union, Gambia Labour Union and Gambia River Trade and Commercial Workers' Union. None has a membership of more than a few hundred.
