THE CAMEROONS
(FORMERLY UNDER UNITED KINGDOM TRUSTEESHIP)

The results of the plebiscites held in February 1961 in the Cameroons under United Kingdom Administration were endorsed by the United Nations General Assembly on 21st April 1961.

United Kingdom Trusteeship of the Northern Cameroons terminated on 1st June 1961 on the territory becoming part of the Federation of Nigeria.

United Kingdom Trusteeship of the Southern Cameroons terminated on 1st October 1961 on the territory joining in a federation with the Cameroun Republic.

Full reports have been submitted each year to the Trusteeship Council; that for 1959 being published by Her Majesty's Stationery Office as Colonial No. 350.
